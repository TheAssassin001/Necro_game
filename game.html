What is Necrowarp?

Necrowarp is a turn-based roguelike focused on exploration, survival, and meaningful choices. The game emphasizes clarity, challenge, and replayability.

Core Gameplay Pillars

  • Turn-based exploration and combat
  • Procedural dungeon generation
  • Resource management and survival
  • Permanent consequences (permadeath, meaningful choices)
  • Minimalist, text-driven interface

Narrative & Tone

Necrowarp presents a mysterious, atmospheric world. The narrative is delivered through concise text, focusing on mood and player discovery rather than exposition.

Inspirations

Inspired by classic roguelikes and minimalist design, Necrowarp draws from games like Cogmind, Larn, and other genre-defining titles.
