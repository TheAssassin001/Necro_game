What is Necrowarp?

Necrowarp is a dark fantasy, turn-based roguelike focused on tactical summoning, dungeon descent, and survival against the forces of good.

Core Gameplay Mechanics

Summon and control abominations, turn-based tactical combat, procedural dungeon exploration, resource management and survival, permanent consequences, and meaningful choices.

Narrative Tone & Inspirations

Necrowarp draws inspiration from classic roguelikes and dark fantasy fiction. The tone is mysterious, atmospheric, and focused on player-driven discovery rather than exposition.
